My Podcasts

Who doesn't love a good Reddit story? My podcast "Huh?" is a podcast that explores and discusses university-related stories sourced from Reddit. With a focus on experiences and challenges, this podcast is a platform for insightful conversations. My cohosts, Kaliyah Lendore, Sean Acceus, Manal Shihadeh and I share our opinions and perspectives on the scenarios discussed. The goal is to inform, entertain, and engage the audience with relatable and thought-provoking content.

Homecoming at FAMU
